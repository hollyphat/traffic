Mobile Traffic Offence and Documentation System

Mobile Traffic Offence and Documentation System

Aims and Objectives

The main aim of this project is to design a system that will enable Federal Road Commission of Nigeria, Osun State Command to document and keep track of all traffic offences committed by road users.

The objectives of the study are as follows:

  1. Design and implementation of a mobile application for Traffic offence documentation System
  2. To produce a system where information and output report will be produced or made available much faster, more accurately and more detailed to the commission and the public by educating motorist and members of the public on the importance of discipline on the highway.
  3. To keep records that are complete, integrated and up to date, also recommending works and devices designed to eliminate or minimize accidents on the highways and advising the State Government and relevant governmental agencies on the localities where such works and devices are required.
